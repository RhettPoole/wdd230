Rental Agreement

A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations. A valid driver's license is required at the time of rental. No one under the age of 12 is allowed to ride on the scooters. Fuel charges are not included. Unlimited mileage is provided. Liability insurance is included, and damage insurance is available for purchase. Free delivery to Cozumel hotels or Airbnbs is available for rentals of 2+ days. Payment methods include Google Pay, Apple Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or Venmo. Rentals may be returned to either physical location.
